About Us

Alfagems Alumni.

Established since 2015

Alfagems Alumni is composed of former students who graduated at Alfagems secondary school. It was established with the mission to promote social interaction and economy amongst youth working for equality and quality education to change lives through life skills by inspiring shared understanding and appreciation between different cultures of preserving environment.

Each year, Alfagems Alumni come back to the school for events or as speakers, the Alumni attend social gatherings and establish and strengthen bonds they made while at ALFAGEMS SECONDARY SCHOOL. For current students, the Alumni provide a strong network for possible jobs and guidance for career and university choices.

As for Alumni, whether it’s connecting with fellow graduates and creating a community beyond your time at Alfagems Alumni, coming back to ALFAGEMS SCHOOL to speak with students or signing up to be a mentor, you’ll find endless benefits of joining our Alumni Network.

Currently, Alfagems Alumni has more than 200 active members who have completed their secondary school at Alfagems in different years.

Alumni Membership

The Alfagems Alumni is run by, and for students who have graduated at Alfagems secondary school. The Alumni is open and free to join for all Alfagems graduated candidates, there are no membership fees or dues. Full membership shall be conveyed to anyone who has graduated from a form four (Form IV) or form six (Form VI) at Alfagems secondary school regardless year of completion.
